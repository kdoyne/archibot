Archibot

GA WDI NYC Jan 14, Project 1

Description

This is an application that is designed for people visiting cities. You can browse by architect and get suggestions of buildings that they have designed. You can add a building of architectural significance and it will show buildings that other people have added in the same area. It will also show photos from instagram taken in that location. Once user has created an account, they can favorite buildings and architects to keep track of them and they can create trips to keep track of the locations that they want to visit together.
